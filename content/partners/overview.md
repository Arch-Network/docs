Arch's partner ecosystem supports the capital markets infrastructure stack with specialized capabilities in yield, custody, risk management, and cross-chain liquidity.

## HoneyB — Yield & Asset Management

HoneyB is Arch's flagship yield partner. Using Arch Lend's API, HoneyB borrows stablecoins against deposited BTC and deploys capital into traditional private credit markets for yield. This is the credit carry trade on Bitcoin — proof that Arch's credit primitives connect to real capital markets.

## Bitfrost — Bridge & Prime Brokerage

Bitfrost provides cross-chain bridge services and prime brokerage infrastructure, enabling liquidity to flow between Bitcoin and other ecosystems when needed.

## Chaos Labs — Oracle Infrastructure

Chaos Labs serves as Arch's oracle provider, delivering the price feed infrastructure that Arch Lend relies on for accurate collateral valuation and liquidation triggers.

## Gauntlet — Risk Management

Gauntlet provides institutional-grade risk management and vault strategy calibration, ensuring Arch Lend's parameters are optimized for market conditions.

## Fordefi & Anchorage — Institutional Custody

Institutional custody solutions that integrate with Arch's wallet infrastructure, providing the compliance and security frameworks institutions require.

## Xverse & Asigna — Wallet Infrastructure

Bitcoin wallet infrastructure enabling direct access to Arch's product suite through Taproot address integration.
