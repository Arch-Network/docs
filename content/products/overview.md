Arch is a vertically integrated super app for Bitcoin capital markets. Rather than building a platform for third parties to create fragmented financial products, Arch internalizes the full stack: trading, lending, portfolio management, and yield — all integrated, all settled natively on Bitcoin.

## Why Vertical Integration

The entity that controls both execution and the risk engine has a structural advantage no one building on just one layer can replicate. When trading (Arch Swap) and lending (Arch Lend) share a closed-loop liquidity system, liquidations execute with the fidelity needed to scale credit. When portfolio management (Arch Prime) sits on top of both, risk is monitored in real-time across all positions.

This is how traditional finance works — prime brokerages don't outsource their risk engine. Arch brings this model to Bitcoin.

## The Products

* **[Arch Swap](./arch-swap)** — Native BTC trading with atomic settlement
* **[Arch Lend](./arch-lend)** — Bitcoin-backed credit with 300ms liquidation fidelity
* **[Arch Prime](./arch-prime)** — Real-time portfolio management dashboard
* **[Yield Vaults](./yield-vaults)** — Automated yield strategies across lending and structured products

## How They Connect

Arch Lend is the foundation. Scalable Bitcoin-backed credit creates the collateral infrastructure that everything else builds on. Arch Swap provides the liquidity and trading layer. Arch Prime gives institutions visibility and control. Yield Vaults deploy capital efficiently. Together, they form the base layer for Bitcoin capital markets.
