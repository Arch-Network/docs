Arch's yield vaults deploy capital across lending markets and structured strategies to generate yield on Bitcoin.

## Core Capabilities

* **Automated strategy execution** across Arch Lend and partner protocols
* **Risk-managed yield** — strategies calibrated to institutional risk tolerances
* **Native BTC yield** — returns denominated in BTC, not inflationary token rewards
* **Transparent performance** — all vault activity visible in Arch Prime

## How Vaults Connect to the Stack

Yield vaults sit on top of Arch Lend and Arch Swap. They deploy capital into lending pools, manage collateral positions, and rebalance through trading — all within Arch's integrated infrastructure.
