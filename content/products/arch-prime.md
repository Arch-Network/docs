Arch Prime is Arch's institutional portfolio management dashboard, providing real-time visibility across all positions, collateral, and yield.

## Core Capabilities

* **Real-time position monitoring** across Arch Swap, Arch Lend, and yield vaults
* **Collateral health tracking** — LTV ratios, liquidation proximity, and margin alerts
* **Yield performance** — track returns across lending positions and vault strategies
* **Risk exposure** — aggregated view of portfolio risk across all Arch products
* **Transaction history** — complete audit trail of all activity settled on Bitcoin
